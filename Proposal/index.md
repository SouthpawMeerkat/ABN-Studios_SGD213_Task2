# Project Overview
[//]: # (This section is an example structure for the proposal to your client.)
Looking for a standard Doom, Halo 1-2, Quake, or Duke Nukem-style FPS. Need some basic enemy AI that walks around and looks at the player when in range (They don’t have to shoot back). The player character needs 2 guns they can switch between, a grenade, and movement. 


## Design
[//]: # (How will you meet the client's brief, their expectations, and their requirements.)
Using component-based programming in Unity Engine, we will compose a template that fits the brief and allow the client to proceed with teaching his class

### Description
[//]: # (This is the elevator pitch, sell the idea)
* 2 guns that the player can switch between and use to fire a bullet
* First gun will be an automatic rifle with a 20 round mag, increased recoil, and bullet spread for group engagements
* The second gun will be a shotgun with a small bullet spread, 4 rounds, high damage, and a long reload time
* Simple player movement, including WASD and at least a jump function
* Enemy AI moving around and facing the player when in a certain proximity (Or potentially line of sight)
* a grenade that the player can throw and will blow up after a set time, dealing damage in an area
